Cooperamos Escuela

Project Overview

Cooperamos Escuela is a landing page designed for a leadership school in San Gil. The goal was to clearly communicate the program’s value and generate trust through structure, testimonials, and a professional visual tone.

I designed the UI and prototype, and implemented the final page using Frappe Builder, adapting the layout to platform constraints while maintaining consistency and clarity.

Key Problems

Lack of early trust signals

The main CTA used the label “Buy”, which implied an in-app checkout. However, the actual flow redirected users to WhatsApp, creating confusion and breaking user expectations.

Conversion Friction

Redirecting users to WhatsApp without clear context introduced hesitation and reduced trust, potentially causing users to abandon the process before contacting the business.

Exploration & Structure

Early iterations explored different content orders and visual directions. While the overall structure remained simple, one key change involved the placement of testimonials. Initially, reviews were positioned later in the page; however, this delayed the introduction of trust signals and relied too heavily on users scrolling before feeling confident.

The structure was adjusted to surface testimonials immediately after the hero section. This allowed social proof to support the value proposition early, helping users feel reassured before engaging with more detailed information about the program.
