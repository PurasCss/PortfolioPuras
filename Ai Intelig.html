AI Intelig

Project Overview

AI Intelig is a landing page concept for an AI startup, created as a personal project to explore and experimentate with a futuristic visual direction and integrate a 3D model.

The main goal was to design a high-impact layout while using the 3D model intentionally supporting the message and structure of the page without overusing it in every section.

I designed the UI and prototype in Figma and built the experience in Dora.

Key Problems

Implement 3D element

The challenge was to integrate a 3D model in a way that supported the brand narrative and user experience, rather than functioning as a purely decorative element.

Visual identity

The interface needed a color system that communicated innovation and trust while remaining accessible and consistent across the product.

Exploration & Structure

Early explorations considered adding notifications and group features to increase engagement. However, due to the simplicity of the product and the external WhatsApp flow, these options risked adding unnecessary complexity.

The focus shifted toward clarity and a linear flow that clearly communicates the next step to the user.

Project Overview

Early explorations centered around the use of a 3D element to reinforce the Al concept. A brain constructed from interconnected dots was chosen as a symbolic representation of intelligence and data. Initially, the idea was to have the 3D model continuously move along the page as the user scrolled. However, this approach risked making the experience feel monotonous and overly dependent on a single visual element.

The direction shifted toward using the 3D model selectively across key sections, such as the hero and supporting areas near the benefits and call-to-action. This allowed the 3D element to enhance immersion and maintain visual interest without distracting from the content or increasing cognitive load.

To support the Al theme and establish credibility, a dark, high-contrast color scheme with neon accents was explored and selected. This visual direction helped communicate innovation and professionalism, while ensuring text remained readable and accessible.g unnecessary complexity. The focus shifted toward clarity and a linear flow that clearly communicates the next step to the user.
